How It Works

Four moves. You're present for two of them.

The whole model in one sentence: you make the decisions, we do the work between them.

01

The handoff call

One structured hour. We learn the business, your numbers, your floor, and your timeline. You hand over read-only access — and that's most of your job done.

You + Us

02

We build & list

Valuation, recast financials, listing copy, metric verification, anonymized placement where the right buyers already look. You approve the final listing with one reply.

Us

03

We screen & negotiate

NDAs out, tire-kickers filtered, proof of funds verified, questions answered from the pack we built. Offers arrive summarized and compared, with our recommendation attached.

Us

04

You sign. We close.

You accept the offer worth taking. We run escrow, asset migration, and the handover period until the funds clear and the keys are theirs.

What We Handle

The 47, organized into six jobs

Prep

Deal Prep & Financial Pack

Valuation, recast P&L, add-back schedule, and verified metrics — the documents that make buyers move fast and negotiate fairly.

Market

Listing & Placement

Copy that sells the business without exposing it, placed anonymously where serious acquirers of your kind of asset already browse.

Screen

Buyer Screening & NDAs

Every enquiry vetted, every NDA executed, proof of funds confirmed before anyone sees your name or your numbers. Your inbox stays yours.

Negotiate

Negotiation & Structuring

We run the back-and-forth, compare offers on price and terms, and structure earnouts or transitions so the headline number survives the fine print.

Close

Escrow & Closing Coordination

Escrow setup, LOI-to-close checklist, and document choreography — managed to the wire so nothing stalls in the final stretch.

Hand over

Migration & Handover

Domains, accounts, integrations, and a structured transition for the new owner — so the deal that closed stays closed.

Before You Hand It Off

The questions everyone asks on the first call

About three hours across the whole engagement: the one-hour handoff call, a handful of approval replies, and the closing signature. Add two minutes each Friday if you read the progress note — which we recommend, because it's usually good news.

We do — all of it. Cold enquiries never reach you, and screened buyers deal with your concierge broker, not your inbox. If you want to meet the finalist before accepting, we'll set that up; plenty of sellers choose to skip even that.

Four sections, every week: what moved, the state of the buyer pipeline, what's planned next, and any decisions we need from you. Most weeks that last section reads "none" — and when it doesn't, the decision arrives pre-summarized with our recommendation.

A flat success fee, quoted at the handoff call and fixed in writing. No retainers, no hourly billing, no charge for the prep work if the deal doesn't close. We get paid when you exit — the incentives stay pointed the same direction as yours.

Yes — that's the default, not an upgrade. The listing is anonymized, buyers sign NDAs before learning your identity or seeing data, and your team, customers, and competitors hear about the sale when you decide they should.
