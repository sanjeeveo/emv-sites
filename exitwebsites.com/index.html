Why Valuation-First

Most brokers list first and hope. We price first and prove.

  • Priced right sells faster. Overpriced listings go stale; underpriced ones leave your money on someone else's table. The valuation kills both risks before launch.
  • A defensible number anchors every negotiation. When a buyer pushes back, we respond with the workings — not with nerves.
  • You decide with the number in hand. Get the valuation, then choose: sell now, fix the drags first, or hold. No pressure to list to justify anyone's time.

The honest math

The estimator above uses the same starting point we do:

12 × monthly net profit × 2.4–3.8

Where your business lands inside that band is the real work. The levers we weigh:

  • Earnings quality and trend, not just the average
  • Traffic mix — owned, organic, paid, platform-dependent
  • Owner hours and how transferable the operation is
  • Niche durability over the next holding period
