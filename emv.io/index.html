The thesis

Own the whole lifecycle of a digital business

Most companies touch a digital business once — broker its sale, run its ads, or sell it software — then move on. EMV owns the entire arc: we help owners know what their business is worth, scale what's working, sell when it's time, and help buyers acquire with confidence.

Every brand in the portfolio is a door into the same engine. A Shopify owner tracking valuation on Dexits becomes a scaling client at Scalifty, lists with Wexits when it's time to exit, and the buyer arrives through Aquire Online. The flywheel compounds — and every participant gets a specialist brand built for exactly their moment.

That's the EMV difference: operator-led, Shopify-native, full-lifecycle. Not a fund. Not an agency. A holding company that runs what it owns.

Operating model

Why a holding company wins

01

Shared deal flow

Sellers, buyers, and scaling clients move between brands instead of leaving the ecosystem. One intake feeds twelve front doors.

02

Shared data

Valuation intelligence from Dexits informs brokerage pricing, scaling benchmarks, and buyer matching across every brand.

03

Shared playbooks

What works in one vertical gets systematized and deployed across the portfolio — distribution, conversion, retention.

Operator discipline

Every brand is run, not just held. Small teams, real P&L ownership, and the founder in the operating seat.
