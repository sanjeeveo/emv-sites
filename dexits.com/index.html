How it works

From install to a growing valuation in three steps

No data exports, no consultants, no guesswork. Dexits plugs into the store you already run.

Connect Shopify in 2 minutes

Install the Dexits app from the Shopify App Store. It uses Shopify's secure OAuth flow with read-only scopes — you never share a password, and you can disconnect in one click.

AI values your store daily

Every 24 hours, Dexits re-runs your valuation across 12 value drivers — revenue quality, margins, repeat rate, traffic concentration, supplier risk and more — so the number moves when your business does.

Follow the playbook to grow it

The assistant turns every weak driver into a specific action with an estimated valuation impact. Ship the change, watch the line move, repeat next week.

Features

Everything you need to manage your store like an asset

Most merchants find out what their store is worth on the day they try to sell it. Dexits makes that number visible — and improvable — every single day.

Daily valuation tracking

A defensible estimate of what your store is worth, refreshed every 24 hours from live Shopify data — not a once-a-year guess.

AI assistant

It watches every driver and surfaces the one action with the highest valuation impact this week — with the estimated dollar effect attached.

Value-driver playbook

Twelve drivers, each with concrete plays: bundles, subscriptions, margin fixes, channel diversification, owner-dependency cleanup.

Benchmarks vs. similar stores

See how your AOV, repeat rate, and margins stack up against stores in your category and size band — and where the gap is costing you value.

Exit-readiness score

A 0–100 score that tells you how sellable your store is today — and exactly which issues would scare a buyer or shrink your multiple.

Valuation history

Every daily valuation is stored. Watch the line climb as you ship improvements — and bring the chart to any buyer or lender conversation.
